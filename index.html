Kanyu Chen

Self-Assistive Technology Researcher

I’m a Doctoral 1st Year Student at Wearable Computing Lab (Prof. Kai Kunze) at Keio University.I received my Master's degree in Geist Lab (Prof. Kai Kunze) Graduate School of Media Design, Keio University, Japan in 2022.I am interested in exploring interaction techniques where people can communicate their embodied experiences to augment their ownself in the fields of emotion regulation, mental health, and human training.To this end, I design wearable interfaces that share one’s embodied experiences across people by means of physiological sensing, data analysis, neuro-network, virtual/augmented reality systems, along with psy-physiological knowledge.
